Vegetable Plants

Advantages

  • Nutrient-dense growth in well-fed soil.
  • High yield with consistent watering and sunlight.
  • Enhanced growth with regular fertilization.

Common Diseases and Cures

  • Aphids: Small insects causing curled leaves. Cure: Use insecticidal soap or neem oil; introduce beneficial insects like ladybugs.
  • Blight: Brown/black spots on leaves and fruit. Cure: Remove affected plant parts, apply fungicide, rotate crops.
  • Powdery Mildew: White powdery coating on leaves. Cure: Improve air circulation, avoid overhead watering, apply fungicide.
  • Root Rot: Mushy, decaying roots. Cure: Use well-drained soil, water sparingly, trim affected roots.
