Succulents


Advantages in Our Environment:

  • Water Conservation: Store water in leaves; highly drought-resistant.
  • Low Maintenance: Thrive in well-drained soil; minimal watering needed.
  • Erosion Control: Roots help stabilize soil; reduce erosion.

Common Diseases:

  • Root Rot: Caused by overwatering; leads to decaying roots.
  • Powdery Mildew: White, powdery fungus on leaves; affects plant health.

Cures for Diseases:

  • Root Rot: Use well-drained soil; water sparingly; remove rotted parts.
  • Powdery Mildew: Improve air circulation; avoid overhead watering; use fungicides if needed.

Cacti


Advantages in Our Environment:

  • Survival in Extreme Conditions: Store water in stems; endure long droughts.
  • Sunlight Utilization: Thrive in intense sunlight; perfect for hot areas.
  • Low Water Use: Require very little water; support conservation.

Common Diseases:

  • Mealybugs: Pests that leave cotton-like residue; weaken the plant.
  • Bacterial Soft Rot: Soft, mushy spots from overwatering; can lead to rot.

Cures for Diseases:

  • Mealybugs: Use insecticidal soap or neem oil; manually remove pests.
  • Bacterial Soft Rot: Increase airflow; reduce watering; cut out affected areas.
