About Us

Imagine being able to diagnose and treat your plants' nutrient deficiencies by simply uploading a photo of a leaf. Our website makes this possible, offering instant, actionable insights that help plants thrive. By uploading a photo of a plant's leaf, users can quickly diagnose which nutrients are missing from the soil. The app analyzes the visual characteristics of the leaf—like color, texture, and pattern—and provides a detailed report with recommendations for the exact fertilizers or soil amendments needed to restore plant health.
